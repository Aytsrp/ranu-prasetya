Who am I?

Ahoy there! My name's Ranu Riang Prasetya. I was born on June 16th, 2006 in Surakarta, Central Java. Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated by computers — from spending hours playing Game House classics to experimenting with my uncle's Python code (he studied IT too, so I had a bit of a head start). Right now, I'm studying for an associate degree (D3) in Informatics Engineering at UNS. I'm naturally curious, quick to adapt, and I enjoy learning new things — especially when it challenges the way I think. I'm also the kind of person who finds excitement in figuring things out, even if it takes time and a few wrong turns. Thanks for taking the time to get to know me — let's see where this journey takes us!
